Green Beans

Home

Description

Spicy roasted green beans with a hint of garlic. This green bean recipe is perfect as a side dish for any meal and can be adjusted to your preferred spice level.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh green beans, trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl, toss the green beans with olive oil, garlic powder, red pepper flakes, salt, and black pepper until evenly coated.
  3. Spread the green beans in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  4. Roast in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, stirring halfway through, until the green beans are tender and slightly crispy.
  5. Remove from the oven and serve immediately as a side dish.

Description

Delicious homemade pizza with fresh ingredients and a crispy crust. This pizza recipe is perfect for any occasion and can be customized with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Add the sliced pepperoni on top of the cheese.
  7. Drizzle olive oil over the pizza and sprinkle with dried oregano.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
